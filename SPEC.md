### Activities Section

Present activities through their benefits rather than as a simple list. Themes to incorporate:

- **Physical Wellness**: Gentle exercises promoting strength and mobility
- **Mental Stimulation**: Puzzles, games, and engaging activities for cognitive health
- **Social Connection**: Group activities that reduce isolation and build friendships
- **Emotional Expression**: Creative outlets like art, music, and gardening
- **Daily Structure**: Consistent routines that provide stability and comfort
- **Independence**: Activities that maintain self-esteem and confidence
- **Personal Growth**: Opportunities for lifelong learning and fulfillment

### DO NOT Include or Mention

- 24-hour care
- Night care
- Memory care / dementia specialty (accept mild cognitive decline but do not advertise)
- Transportation services
- Respite care / short-term stays

## Design Direction

### Tone
- **Warm and homey**, not clinical
- **Traditional/classic** aesthetic
- Emphasize "home" over "facility"
- Simple, clean design matching the line-art logo style

### Visual Style
- Colors derived from logo (cream background, warm browns)
- Serif or elegant fonts for headings
- Clean sans-serif for body text
- Generous whitespace
- Soft, welcoming imagery

## Technical Requirements

- Single-page website with anchor navigation
- No external libraries (native HTML, CSS, JavaScript only)
- Mobile responsive
- GitHub Pages deployment
- Formspree for contact form
- All asset paths relative
